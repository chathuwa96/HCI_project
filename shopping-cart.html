Payment Methods Accepted

At Micro Center stores and Micro Center Online, you can shop using your MasterCard, VISA, American Express, Discover® Network Card, Diners Club, Micro Center Insider™ Credit Card and debit cards. Micro Center and Micro Center Online are based in the state of Ohio, USA.

* The Micro Center Insider® Credit Card is issued by Wells Fargo Bank, N.A. For your protection, Micro Center is no longer allowing Micro Center Insider® Credit Card transactions without your actual card being present. Please bring your Micro Center Insider® Credit Card into the store to enjoy our promotional offers.

When using a debit card as payment, the card is charged immediately following the authorization. The Issuing Bank determines when to release the hold on the dollar amount when merchandise is not shipped (backorder) or the order is cancelled.
